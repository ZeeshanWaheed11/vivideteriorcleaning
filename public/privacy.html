Privacy Policy

VIVID Exterior Cleaning (“VIVID,” “we,” “our,” or “us”) respects the privacy of every customer, website visitor, and community partner. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, who we share it with, and what rights you have. This policy applies to vividexteriorcleaning.co, our online forms, email, text messages, and any other interaction where this Privacy Policy is referenced.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information in the following ways:

  • Information you provide directly: name, company, email address, phone number, service address, project details, photos you upload, payment details, and messages you send us.
  • Automatic information: IP address, device type, browser type, referring URLs, page views, and cookies that help us remember your preferences and improve site performance.
  • Information from third parties:
    • scheduling platforms, payment processors, marketing partners, and review platforms when you interact with our profiles.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Respond to inquiries, provide quotes, schedule services, and manage your account.
  • Send appointment confirmations, service updates, invoices, and safety reminders.
  • Improve our website, marketing, and customer experience.
  • Comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce agreements.
  • Share testimonials, images, or reviews (with your permission) to highlight completed projects.

3. Sharing Your Information

We do not sell or rent your personal information. We only share it with:

  • Trusted service providers such as scheduling platforms, payment processors, technology vendors, and vetted partners who help us run our business.
  • Regulators, fire marshals, or insurance providers when documentation is requested for compliance.
  • Other third parties when required by law, subpoena, or court order, or to protect our rights and safety.

4. Cookies & Tracking Technologies

We use cookies, pixels, and analytics tools to remember your preferences, improve performance, and measure marketing effectiveness. You can manage cookies through your browser settings or decline tracking where available.

5. Text Messaging and Email Communications

When you provide a phone number or email address, you agree we may contact you regarding scheduling, reminders, or service updates. Message and data rates may apply. You may opt-out by replying “STOP” to texts or by clicking “unsubscribe” in emails.

6. Data Retention and Security

We keep service records, invoices, and compliance documentation for as long as required to provide services, maintain legal records, or comply with NFPA-96 and insurance standards. We use physical, technical, and administrative safeguards to protect your information, though no system is 100% secure.

7. Your Rights

You may request access, updates, or deletion of your personal information by emailing privacy@vividexteriorcleaning.co. We will honor applicable legal requests within a reasonable timeframe.

8. Children’s Privacy

Our services are intended for adults and commercial businesses. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with the “Last Updated” date. Continuing to use our services after updates means you accept the revised policy.
